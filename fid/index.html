Fuck It Voucher - Terms & Conditions

A special pass for parents to grant their child a well-deserved break.

1. Eligibility

This voucher is issued to a parent or guardian who may grant their child **one (1) day off** from school.

2. Usage

Parents **MUST** allow their child to redeem this voucher under the following conditions:

  • Only one voucher per academic year per child.
  • Cannot be used on test/exam days.
  • Must notify the school at least 24 hours in advance.

3. Expiration

The voucher expires at the end of the academic year and does not roll over.

4. No Refunds or Exchanges

Once used, the voucher cannot be transferred, exchanged, or refunded.

5. School Notification

Parents must inform the school in accordance with attendance policies. The school reserves the right to deny the request if it conflicts with critical academic activities.

6. Usage Note

The child must complete the following chores on their day off:

  • Make their bed and tidy their room.
  • Do the dishes and washing.
  • Take out the bins and recycling.
  • Complete any outstanding homework.
  • Help with 1 household task of the parent's choice.
